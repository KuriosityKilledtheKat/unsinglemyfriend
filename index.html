what even is this??

unsingle my friend is a social matchmaking experiment where you, the friend of someone who is single, can do something about their unsingle-ing. we turn your single friend into a trading card, you bring that card to a chaotic IRL meetup, and then you trade cards with other friends of singles. if they have a friend that's willing to go through all that trouble for them, they must be ok, right?

how it works

  1. have a single friend.
  2. be tired of them being single.
  3. want to do something about it.
  4. fill out this form.
  5. wait for us to turn your friend into a collectible trading card.
  6. get invited to an event where you channel your inner Chinese mother at a marriage market. (we will invite you)
  7. get your trading card pack of your Single Friend.
  8. meet other desperate friends of Single people -- and collect other singles like Pokémon.
  9. convince your friend to go on a date with your best “catches.”
